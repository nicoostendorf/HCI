FAQ

  • How can I contact a tutor?

    Press "Get help" button under assignment slides to go to the chat with a tutor.

  • How can I go through slides?

    Press the arrows on the left/right side of a slides-carousel.

  • How can I log out?

    In the top right corner you can find the Logout button, which you have to press in order to log out.

  • Where can I ask a question, not relevant to the slides?

    Contact us under the following email address: help@stack-Overflow.de
